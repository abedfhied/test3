  • About Us

    We are an international public benefit educational corporation and also a global training and learning platform, incorporated in the United States of America with well-established roots in India. We have offices and associates in dozens of countries worldwide. We are a purpose-driven educational corporation that has been established to fill the existing void in the global education system and foster educational excellence. We have a highly qualified and experienced team of professionals and specialists from the United States, India, Afghanistan, CIS countries, Africa, and the Arab world. Our team comprises of eminent educationists such as educational consultants, career counselors, student support experts, medical & dental doctors, former university rectors, and administrators, among others. Our corporation also has diversified professionals with varied academic backgrounds and expertise united by a strong set of values, commitment to excellence, and dedication to clients and customers. For over 35 years, we have been providing customer-driven, result-oriented end-to-end solutions and services for a large customer base, including students, trainees, academic institutions, private enterprises, hospitals, research organizations, public and charity organizations, etc. We are a parent-subsidiary corporation that owns a number of subsidiaries in the health and education domain.

  • VISION

    To be a global education industry leader and to promote excellence in education through training, teaching, and research.

  • MISSION

    Our mission is to become one of the most highly regarded global educational organizations and be recognized among clients, customers, and the public as a global promoter of educational excellence.
